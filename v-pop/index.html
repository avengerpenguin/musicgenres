V-pop

V-pop (Vietnamese: nhạc pop Việt Nam, nhạc trẻ or nhạc xanh), an abbreviation for Vietnamese popular music, is a music genre covering Vietnamese pop music from the 1990s to the present day.

Influences

  • Rhythm and blues
  • Bolero
  • Pop music
  • Rock music
