Grindie

Grindie, also known as grime-indie or grime indie, is a form of music that emerged in early 2006. It is a fusion of indie rock or alternative rock and grime music.

Influences

  • Indie rock
  • Industrial rock
  • Alternative hip hop
  • Alternative rock
  • UK garage
  • Hip hop music
  • Pop music
