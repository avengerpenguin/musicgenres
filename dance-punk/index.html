Dance-punk

Dance-punk (also known as disco-punk, punk-funk or techno-punk) is a post-punk subgenre that emerged in the late 1970s, and is closely associated with the disco, post-disco and new wave movements.

Influences

  • Punk rock
  • Post-disco
  • Funk
  • Disco
  • Post-punk
  • Krautrock
  • No wave

Derivatives

  • New rave
  • Screamo
