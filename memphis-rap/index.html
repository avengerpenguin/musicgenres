Memphis rap

Memphis rap, also known as Memphis hip hop or Memphis horrorcore, is a regional subgenre of hip hop music that originated in Memphis, Tennessee in the mid-late 1980s.

Influences

  • Horrorcore
  • Gangsta rap
  • Southern hip hop

Derivatives

  • Phonk
  • Trap music
  • Crunk
