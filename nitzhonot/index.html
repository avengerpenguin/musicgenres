Nitzhonot

Nitzhonot (Hebrew: נצחונות, “victories”) is a form of Goa trance, fused with Uplifting trance, that emerged during the mid-late 1990s in Israel.

Influences

  • Psychedelic rock
  • Electronic body music
  • Trance music
  • Acid house
  • Goa trance
  • New beat
