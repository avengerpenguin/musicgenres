Dunedin sound

The Dunedin sound was a style of indie pop music created in the southern New Zealand university city of Dunedin in the early 1980s.

Influences

  • Psychedelic pop
  • Punk rock
  • Garage rock
  • Post-punk
  • Indie pop

Derivatives

  • Indie rock
  • Dolewave
