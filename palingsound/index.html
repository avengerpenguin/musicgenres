Palingsound

Palingsound (/pɑːlɪŋsaʊnt/; lit. ‘European eel sound’) is the unique musical sound attributed to a subgenre of Dutch pop music called palingpop. It is sometimes used to refer to the palingpop genre as a whole.

Influences

  • Rhythm and blues
  • Rock music
  • Nederpop
